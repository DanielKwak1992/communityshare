What's a K-12 Teacher?

K-12 teachers are individuals who teach daily in a school classroom setting. If you are a school administrator or an educator who teaches/mentors youth in "informal" learning environments (e.g. afterschool programs, nonprofit educational programs, homeschooling) who wants to bring in a community partner, please sign up as a "K-12 classroom teacher." We know this is clunky and we promise in the next version of CommunityShare to make it easier for different types of educators to identify themselves when they sign up.

If you wear dual hats -- working sometimes are an educator and other times serving a community partner -- we encourage you to make one profile as an educator and another as a community partner. For example, you might currently teach 8th grade Art but in "your previous life" you worked as a graphic designer and are willing to share your graphic design expertise with others.
